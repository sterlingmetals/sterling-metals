About the Yard

Sterling Metals is a stainless steel plant operating out of Rajasthan Udyog Nagar on GT Karnal Road, supplying industrial and commercial buyers across Delhi with graded stainless material.

The facility is built around dependable turnaround: clients come for consistent grades, accurate cuts and stock that's ready when the job needs it — not the other way around. That reliability is what keeps the yard busy around the clock.

As a working distributor rather than a showroom, Sterling Metals serves fabricators, contractors and manufacturers who need volume, consistency and a straight answer on lead times.
